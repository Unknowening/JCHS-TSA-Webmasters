About:

Electric Cars

Being some of the most revolutionary products created in the 21st century, electric cars are a category of vehicles that run primarily on the power of energy to carry out functions. By replacing gasoline, these vehicles are minimizing the danger caused by the emission of harmful gases by standard cars and limit worldwide consumption of the quickly disappearing oil reserves. Increase energy security, improve fuel economy, lower fuel costs, and reduce emissions.

History

Our founder Jón Dottir founded THOR in 2003 when he noticed the harm that cars running on fossil fuels were pushing onto the environment. He himself noted the problems and limited himself to bicycles and public transportation, but then realized that the change had to be on a large scale. Thus came THOR, one of the most popular and powerful electric cars to reach the worldwide market.
